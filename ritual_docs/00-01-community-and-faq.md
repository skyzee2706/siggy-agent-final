# Ritual Community Guide & Ecosystem FAQs

## What is Ritual?
Ritual is the most expressive blockchain in existence, with a single focus: enrich what users can do on-chain today to attract the users of tomorrow.

## How does Ritual compare to other blockchains?
Ritual moves beyond merely scaling existing workloads to fundamentally re-imagine on-chain computation and enrich user functionality. Key differentiators include:
- **Native, Heterogeneous Compute**: Support for AI Inference, ZK Proving & Verification, TEE execution, and future paradigms via a forward-compatible architecture.
- **Flexible Verification**: Built with modular computational primitives.
- **Node Specialization**: Enabling diverse participants and workload specialization.
- **Resonance**: State-of-the-art execution pricing via a novel fee market design.

## How does Ritual compare to other Crypto x AI projects?
Ritual's modular architecture natively incorporates model training workloads, inference networks for web2 and on-chain, agent frameworks, IP & Model provenance, and seamless integration with privacy-preserving solutions into a cohesive, optimized system.

## What can I build on the Ritual Chain?
The expressive nature of Ritual enables a wide variety of advanced applications, including:
- **AI-Pegged Stablecoins**: Transparent stablecoins, backed by verifiable AI models.
- **Prediction Markets**: Robust prediction markets with automated market creation, optimized liquidity management, and enshrined oracles.
- **Basis Trading Protocols**: Efficient trading protocols featuring automated risk management, optimized execution, and dynamic liquidity scaling.
- **Smart Agents**: Verifiable agents with provable autonomy and enhanced on-chain capabilities.
- **Borrowing & Lending Platforms**: Next-generation DeFi lending with dynamic parameter management, advanced risk assessment, and optimized liquidations.

## What is EVM++?
EVM++ sidecars are modular extensions to the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) that enable specialized computation (such as AI inference or ZK proving) while maintaining full EVM compatibility. They handle complex operations asynchronously and securely provide verified results back to the main chain.

# Discord Community Structure & Roles

Our community is built on contribution, loyalty, and human-first innovation. In Ritual, **roles are earned, not given**. Asking for roles may result in XP loss or banishment. Trust in the process, contribute meaningfully, and you will be recognized. 

## Notification Roles
Opt in to receive specific updates via the Server Guide:
- `@Events`: For IRL and Online community building events.
- `@Workshops`: For IRL and Online developer workshop events.
- `@Official`: Official announcements from the Ritual Foundation, Ritual Labs, and project leaders.
- `@DevUpdates`: Technical updates specifically for the developer community.
- `@Community`: General updates for the broader Ritual community.

## Community Progression Roles
- `@Initiate`: You’re new to the community and have completed the verification process. Welcome!
- `@Ascendant`: You have pledged to Ritual, marking the start of your community journey.
- `@ritty bitty`: You're a recognized "little bitty baby Ritualist" on the right path, but with a long way to go.
- `@ritty`: A long-term, loyal community member with strong conviction for what we are building. You will be invited to an exclusive Telegram chat.
- `@Ritualist`: The highest honor in our community. Becoming a Ritualist means you have authentically demonstrated your commitment and value to the project.
- `@Mage`: A Ritualist with a specialized focus. Mages create impactful written content, art, or memes that help grow the community.
- `@Radiant Ritualist`: A super rare "Golden Ritualist" role, reserved exclusively for true community leaders.
- `@Forerunner`: The earliest supporters who have been here since the time before Ritual.

## Blessings and Curses (Community Magic Commands)
To bless is to curse, to curse is to bless—embrace the Ritual, for fate is woven in both. Both Blessings and Curses provide value in the community ecosystem. 

You can use the following bot commands in Discord to evoke community magic:
- `/bless`: Give a friend a blessing.
- `/curse`: Give a friend a curse.
- `/stats`: Check your current Blessing/Curse statistics.
- `?confess`: Use the `#confessions` channel to confess your "syns".
- `?sacrifice`: Sacrifice your curses to receive an omen.
- `?oracle`: Spend your blessings to call forth a message from the beyond.
