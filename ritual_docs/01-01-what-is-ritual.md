## Why does the world need another blockchain?

Behind every magnitude increase in blockchain users lies an innovation that
enables net-new behavior for the masses. In 2009, Bitcoin enabled anyone,
anywhere to send and receive money without relying on centralized
intermediaries. In 2015, Ethereum introduced smart contracts, enabling
developers to build permissionless, composable financial applications.

Yet, somewhere along the way, we lost sight of building for the **net-new
user**, of expanding the status quo of what could be built with truly trustless
and expansive computation. Today's blockchains prioritize latency and
throughput, optimizing for existing users rather than innovating. While these
performance improvements matter, they merely scale current use cases instead of
unlocking transformative new ones.

**Ritual was born from a single focus: enrich what users can do on-chain *today*
to attract the users of *tomorrow*.**

Our expedition began at the
[intersection of Crypto and Artificial Intelligence](/overview/crypto-x-ai). Two
things are simultaneously true: it has never been easier for developers to build
with AI, crafting *dynamic* and *personalized* experiences, and it has never
been more important to ensure that the AI technologies we adopt remain open,
credibly neutral, and censorship resistant. To that extent, Ritual makes smart
contracts *actually smart*, allowing users to natively tap into on-chain AI,
backed by the same trustless properties of modern blockchains.

However, Ritual isn't just focused on Crypto x AI. By optimizing our underlying
architecture for any type of heterogeneous compute, Ritual hyperscales the
expressive computation that can be performed on a blockchain. Ritual is the best
venue not just for developers looking to harness
[AI, ZK, or TEEs](/whats-new/evm++-sidecars/overview) in their applications, but
also for those experimenting at the edges of the unknown, defining new ways to
consume underlying infrastructure.

Our architecture [efficiently prices](/whats-new/resonance), orchestrates, and
exposes all types of compute, current or future, enabling Ritual to become the
schelling point for all of web3.

Not only will end-users consume applications natively through Ritual, but other
blockchains themselves symbiotically benefit through Ritual. High-performance
chains that require a [prover network](/beyond-crypto-x-ai/prover-networks)
(MegaETH, Movement, etc.), modern
[Rollup-as-a-Service](/beyond-crypto-x-ai/l2-raas) offerings that require
efficient execution of heterogeneous compute (Conduit, Caldera, etc.), and the
[totality of IP](/beyond-crypto-x-ai/model-marketplace), monetized via a
verifiable marketplace (Story, Sentient, etc.) will all use Ritual.
