## Ritual as a blockchain backend

Not only will end-users consume applications natively through Ritual, but other
blockchains themselves symbiotically benefit by tapping into Ritual compute. Via
general message passing or [Infernet](/architecture/infernet-to-chain),
blockchain networks themselves can outsource their heterogeneous computation to
optimized Ritual architecture.

We expect initial consumers of this pattern to be high-performance chains that
require a [prover network](/beyond-crypto-x-ai/prover-networks), modern
[Rollup-as-a-Service](/beyond-crypto-x-ai/l2-raas) offerings that require
efficient execution of heterogeneous compute, and the
[totality of IP](/beyond-crypto-x-ai/model-marketplace), monetized via a
verifiable marketplace.
